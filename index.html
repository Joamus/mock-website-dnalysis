Next Generation Sequencing

Sequencing DNA has never been easier

Next-generation sequencing (NGS) is a massively parallel sequencing technology that offers ultra-high throughput, scalability, and speed.

The technology is used to determine the order of nucleotides in entire genomes or targeted regions of DNA or RNA. NGS has filled that gap and become an everyday tool to address these questions.
